Lasagna

Description

When John Chandler submitted this lasagna recipe more than 20 years ago, he had no idea how successful it would become.

"World's Best Lasagna" racks up more than 7 million views per year and has ranked among the most popular lasagna recipes on the internet for two decades

Ingredients

  • meat
  • onion, garlic
  • tomato products
  • sugar
  • spices, seasoning
  • lasagna noodles
  • cheese (parmesan, mozzarella, ricotta)
  • egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
